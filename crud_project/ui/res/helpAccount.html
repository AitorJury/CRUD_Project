Account Management Guide

Welcome to the bank account management section. From this window, you can securely manage your financial products.

1. Viewing Accounts

The main table displays all your associated accounts. You can see the unique ID, description, account type, and balances.

  • Balance: Reflects the current available funds. If the number appears in red, your account is overdrawn.
  • Credit Line: Only available for CREDIT type accounts.

2. Creating a New Account

  1. Click the "Create Account" button. An empty row will be added at the end of the table.
  2. Enter the Description and the Initial Balance directly into the cells.
  3. Select the Account Type (Standard or Credit).
  4. Click "Confirm" to save to the server or "Cancel" to abort the process.

3. Editing and Restrictions

Important (Business Rule): Once an account is created, for security reasons, you may only modify the Description and the Credit Line (if it is a credit account).

4. Deleting Accounts

To remove an account, select it and click the "Delete Account" button or use the context menu (right-click).

  • Restriction: Only accounts with no registered movements can be deleted to maintain data integrity.

5. Viewing Movements

Select an account and click "View Movements". A modal window will open with the detailed history of deposits and payments for that specific account.

6. Keyboard Shortcuts

  • F1: Open this help guide.
  • Del: Delete the selected account.
  • Ctrl+S: Securely sign out.
